BACKGROUND DESIGN + GEN Z VIBE (CRITICAL UPGRADE):

1. SLIDE ORIENTATION (STRICT):

* ALL slides must be LANDSCAPE (16:9 ratio)
* Optimized for projector / desktop viewing
* No mobile-first layouts
* No vertical/portrait compositions

---

2. BACKGROUND SYSTEM (NOT PLAIN WHITE):

Do NOT use empty white backgrounds.

Instead, use subtle, animated background elements:

* Soft gradient overlays (white → very light blue tint)
* Abstract shapes (circles, blobs, lines)
* Floating UI fragments (LinkedIn-style cards blurred in background)
* Light grid or dot patterns (very subtle)

Color usage:

* Keep mostly white
* Add hints of #0A66C2 (very soft, low opacity)
* Occasional neutral gradients

---

3. ANIMATED BACKGROUND ELEMENTS:

Add slow, subtle motion:

* Floating shapes (move slightly up/down)
* Parallax layers (background moves slower than content)
* Soft glowing elements
* Animated gradient shift (very slow)

Animation rules:

* Duration: 6–12 seconds (loop)
* Very subtle (no distraction)
* Low opacity

---

4. GEN Z VISUAL STYLE (IMPORTANT):

Tone should feel:

* Modern
* Slightly playful
* Clean but not boring

Add:

* Rounded blobs
* Soft glow accents
* Thin lines / connectors
* Micro UI stickers (like “+ Connect”, “Like”, etc.)

BUT:

* Avoid clutter
* Avoid meme style
* Avoid loud neon

---

5. DEPTH & LAYERS:

Each slide should have 3 layers:

* Background (animated shapes)
* Mid layer (soft UI or pattern)
* Foreground (main content)

Use blur + opacity to create depth.

---

6. CARD STYLING UPGRADE:

* Slight glassmorphism (optional):
  backdrop-filter: blur(10px)
* Soft borders
* Floating effect

---

7. CONSISTENCY:

* Background style must feel connected across slides
* Slight variation per slide, but same design language
* Do NOT randomly change themes

---

8. DO NOT:

* Do NOT keep plain white empty slides
* Do NOT add heavy gradients or dark themes
* Do NOT distract from content
* Do NOT over-animate

---

FINAL RESULT:

Slides should feel:

* Alive (subtle motion)
* Modern Gen Z
* Premium but not corporate
* Clean but not boring

The background should enhance the experience, not compete with content.
